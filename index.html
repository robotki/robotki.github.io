Aquatic Robot

AVEXIS

The University of Manchester, UK

The Aqua Vehicle for Exploration and In-Situ Sensing (AVEXIS) is a small underwater vehicle that been designed to survey and monitor hazardous environments, ensuring a more efficient decommissioning process and protecting human workers from harm.

Digital Asset

PointCloud Streaming

RAIN Hub, UK

This Unity asset allows the streaming, rendering and VR visualisation of PointCloud2 data using ROS and Unity3D. It can also replay and visualise elements from past robot LIDAR missions such as robot path history and significant events.

Research Group

Smart Systems Group

Heriot-Watt University, UK

Influencing future product development, business models, resilience of critical infrastructure and exploration within extreme environments.

Simulation Environment

Nuclear Facility

The University of Manchester, UK

This robot simulator allows the operation of digital robotic twins on certain environments in nuclear decomissioning. Developed using Unity3D and ROS, it can simulate land, water, and air dynamics as well as effects of radiation on sensor health.
